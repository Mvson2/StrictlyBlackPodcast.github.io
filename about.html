ABOUT US

JAYSON SHEPARD - PRODUCER & HOST

Born and raised in Memphis, TN.

He is currently finishing his 12th grade year at Overton High School. He engages in many art forms such as dance, singing, drama, music production, and graphic design. Jayson Produced the Strictly Black theme song.

 Jayson was also inspired to start Strictly Black alongside his cohost Taijah as a way to share laughter and education about black culture. His vision is to create a platform accessible to people of all colors and ages.
